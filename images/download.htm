Facilities

Seminar Hall: Conferences, Technical Workshop..

Home / Facilities

Seminar Hall:

Conferences, Technical Workshop and Seminars are day in and day out activities of the institute. The institute has a well furnished air conditioned Seminar Hall with about 200 seating capacity. The hall is also equipped with an overhead projector, an LCD Projector and Audio-Video facilities.

Canteen:

Apart from the basic amenities of the hostel, the institute having a good cafeteria named “Campus Canteen”, which provides snacks, tea & coffee, soft drinks, lunch and fast food at reasonable costs.

Sports Complex:

Sports are one of the key factors in the overall development of students personality and character building. In view of this fact, institute provides excellent sporting facilities for indoor and outdoor games. Apart from the games ,the sport arena also equipped with latest form of Gymnasium, Yoga facilities for enacting the other facets of the life. The unique feature of the sports arena is, one of the biggest turf wicket cricket ground in the South Gujarat region of domestic level along with the stands.

Internet:

SVMIT is equipped with Internet lab in which students and faculty member can communicate across the world. students can access Internet for having information based on any subject or project on the web.

Following Facilities are available near by area of SVMIT College

  •  AXIS BANK Main Branch with ATM in College Campus
  • Petrol Pump in front of College Campus
  • Shopping Center (Stationary,  Xerox, Others etc.) in front of College (At Walk-able Distance)
  • Railway Station (At Walk-able Distance)
  • Railway, Road and Auto Rickshaw are easily available from SVMIT college
  • Medical Facilities are available near by area of SVMIT College
